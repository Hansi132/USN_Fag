About

TechCon is a Norwegian consulting company, founded in 2019.

Since our release, we already have over 50 employees all around Norway, who strive everyday to meet our clients expectations and quality requirements.

Some of our services and products have already reached the international market, and are being used by small and big companies in Sweden, Norway, Poland, Germany, England and Australia.

TechCon focuses on developing and delivering products of high quality that are popular on the market, such as:

  • Stock automates
  • Working environment products
  • Pallet rackings

We also deliver services, such as storage solutions, services and industry solutions.
